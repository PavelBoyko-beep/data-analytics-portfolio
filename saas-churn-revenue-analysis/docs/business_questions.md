# Business Questions

## Business Problem

A fictional SaaS company wants to understand why customers cancel their subscriptions, how churn affects recurring revenue, and which customer segments, plans, and behaviors are connected with higher retention or higher churn.

The goal of this analysis is to help the business answer three main questions:

1. Where does the company generate recurring revenue?
2. Which customers are more likely to churn?
3. What actions can help improve retention and protect MRR?

This project focuses on SQL-based business analysis, basic data cleaning in Python, and dashboard reporting in Power BI.

## Key Business Questions

1. How does Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) change over time?
2. What is the estimated Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)?
3. How many active customers does the company have each month?
4. How many new customers are acquired each month?
5. How many customers churn each month?
6. What is the monthly churn rate?
7. Which subscription plans generate the most revenue?
8. Which subscription plans have the highest churn rate?
9. Which countries or regions have the highest customer churn?
10. Do customers with more support tickets churn more often?
11. How does churn differ by customer tenure?
12. Which customer cohorts show better retention over time?
